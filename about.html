APPENDIX :

We embarked on our road to excellence in association with Aptech Computer Education in 1998.. Some of our major landmark achievements in our pursuit of excellence include:

Best centre award for excellence – 2003

National award for best system implementation-2006
We commenced our journey towards academic excellence with De-zone, the authorized distance education study centre for various UGC-recognized UG, PG & Diploma courses and three different established Government Universities

We the Dezone first introduced the idea of having a distance education centre in Malabar area in early 2001 as Mr. Mustafa took initiation and started an off campus study Centre of Mahatma Gandhi university of Kotayam under the banner of Regional Management College.

Manonmanium Sundarnar university ,one of the very well known university which is situated at Tirunelveli recognized by UGC,DEC,NAAC,AICTE and Kerala PSC.

Bharathiar University which is ranked in India’s top two position and affiliated with all nominities like, UGC DEC NAAC AICTE approval.

Periyar University which is also is well versed university in all the distant courses.

Our state-of-the-art study centres are located at three locations across the Malabar region namely Perintalmanna ,Tirur (Malappuram District), Calicut Palakkad and Cochin. We intend to expand our gamut of operations shortly to other GCC countries like including UAE and Qatar.
